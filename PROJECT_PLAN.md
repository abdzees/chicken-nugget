# Her Little Meadow by the Sea — Project Plan

> Recovered from the previous chat session (workspace was renamed from "QR Code Project" to "BunnyProject").
> A small, interactive, handmade-feeling web gift. Warm, cozy, playful, calming, personal, slightly magical — never dramatic or cheesy.

## Tech
- Plain **HTML + CSS + JavaScript** (no frameworks).
- SVG + CSS animations preferred. Mobile responsive, accessible, smooth performance, clean/maintainable architecture.

## The concept: "Her Little Meadow by the Sea"
Blue ink blooms open a paper-sketch meadow at the ocean's edge. An invisible pencil draws *her* as a soft plushie bunny. A swan glides on the water. She plants blue tulips and shares grapes/strawberries with the bunny; hidden in the scene are a MANAS cookie and a tiny plushie. When she's done, a soft firework rises and its sparks settle into stars that spell something just for her.

## Locked-in decisions
- **Visual style:** Notebook doodle / pastel sketchbook, off-white paper texture, blue ink lines, soft pastel fills, wobbly hand-drawn lines. Hidden day → night toggle for magic.
- **Passcode:** `2007` (on the minimalist landing page).
- **Entry transition:** The original blue tulip — but instead of filling the screen, it grows and its petals **part like curtains** to reveal the meadow (loses the melodrama).
- **Character:** A plushie-soft bunny (rounded, stitched-seam look) with a **"Chicken Nugget" nametag**. She refers to herself as a bunny. Drawn live by an "invisible pencil."
- **Main interaction:** Plant blue tulips + share grapes/strawberries with the bunny.
- **Light objective:** Find hidden **iron supplements** scattered in the scene (a gentle, loving nod to her iron deficiency — never a health note) to unlock the finale.
- **Finale:** A soft, hand-drawn **firework** rises; its sparks drift up and settle into **stars that form a constellation** spelling a short word (her name or brief phrase).
- **Final note (discovered at the end):**
  > "You are the best and I pray Allah always keeps you happy. Never let anyone yuck your yum. I can't wait to get to know you more."
  - ("Never let anyone yuck your yum" = don't let others look down on / criticize what you genuinely enjoy.)

## Her preferences woven into the design
- **Blue** → her color; leads everything (blue ink bloom, blue tulips, blue-hour skies).
- **Stars + fireworks** → the finale (gentle firework → stars → constellation).
- **Swans** → a swan glides across the pond/ocean edge (calm idle animation). Easter egg: on repeat visits a **second swan** appears (swans pair for life — quietly romantic, never stated).
- **Tulips** → blue tulips are what she plants in the meadow.
- **The ocean** → soft shoreline at the bottom, distant gentle waves, swan floats there.
- **Plushies** → bunny looks plushie-soft; hidden tiny plushie bunny in a corner that squeaks/hugs when clicked.
- **MANAS dark choc chip chunk cookies** → a hidden cookie; find it and the bunny happily nibbles it (hand-letter tiny "MANAS" on a little bag).
- **Grapes / strawberries / corn** → foods she shares with the bunny (energy burst: soft blush + happy hop).
- **Iron deficiency** → handled gently as the hidden iron-supplement objective.

## Ambient sound (optional, muted by default)
Birds, wind, soft piano, pencil-drawing sounds, distant waves, tiny bells.

## Easter eggs
- Hidden flower in a corner reveals a secret note.
- Day → night toggle (borrows the "Night Garden" magic).
- "Remembers her" on repeat visits (second swan).
- Hidden plushie bunny, hidden MANAS cookie.
- Constellation spelling a short word.
