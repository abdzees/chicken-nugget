made just for you

i hope you always remember how wonderful you are,

i pray that Allah always keeps you happy, gives you peace, and surrounds you with people who appreciate you for who you truly are,

never let anyone make you feel like the things that bring you joy are silly or too much, never let anyone yuck your yum, because the little things that make you smile are a part of what makes you special,

i cant wait to get to know you more, to learn about the things you love, the things that make you laugh, your little habits, and all the small things that make you who you are,

i hope life is always gentle with you, and i hope you always know that you deserve happiness

— abd
